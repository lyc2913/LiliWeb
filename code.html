What code can do but human cannot?

P5JS was used to create a wave effect from randomly colored blocks, using light and shadow to represent the wave effect. The light and shadow can be expressed using trigonometric functions representing the peaks and troughs of a wave.

Endless

The first one is the endless strip of Mobius. P5JS provide me with basic graphics and the most essential twists of the Mobius strip, and I can draw the shape of the Mobius strip on the canvas using the mouse. The second picture is the endless spiral tunnel. P5JS provide me with basic graphics and circular emission from the center to the outside, and I can draw the depth of field effect of the tunnel in the canvas through mouse drawing. The third picture combines the drawing logic of the first Mobius strip with Coca Cola bottles, manually drawing a shape similar to the second spiral tunnel, showing the endless feeling of Coca Cola.
